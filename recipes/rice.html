Fluffy Rice Recipe

This is a description of how to make Fluffy Rice

This is how to make nice and fluffy rice which can be added to any meal. Chicke and rice, fried rice, a burrito, or anything. We won't be seasoning the rice in this recipe, just making the base rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup white or brown rice
  • Tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Bring the 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add the cup of rice to the boiling water
  3. Cover and let water and rice cook on medium heat for 30 minutes
  4. Make sure to periodically stir
  5. Take rice off heat and let sit for 5 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
