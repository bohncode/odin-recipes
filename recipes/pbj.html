Homemade PbJ Sandwhich

This is a description of how to make PbJ Sandwhich

This classic peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is simple and easy to make, so easy that even a dummy could make it. Seriously, it is ridiculously easy to make and only takes a few steps and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Get 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on the bread slices
  3. Spread jelly on the bread slices
  4. Place the 2 pieces of bread on top of eachother
  5. Enjoy!
